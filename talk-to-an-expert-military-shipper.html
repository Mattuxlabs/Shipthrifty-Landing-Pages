Shpthrifty.com is a Free shipping tool. We don't charge a user fee or monthly fees to use the application. It is one of the ways we give back. The pricing that comes up on the system is what you will pay when you check out. Insurance is an optional add on. Enter accurate box dimensions and weights, and you're good to go.
Most people get boxes from the carriers through the website or physical location. Boxes can also be ordered online or picked up at local retailers who carry shipping supplies and local home improvement stores. Carrier branded boxes can only be used with the specified carrier.
Shipthrifty can be used to create shipping labels for any shipping you need to do. Whether you're sending packages to family members, needing a turn-key business solution, or sending packages to an APO, FPO, DPO, we have the tools to make it easy to do and save you some money.
You don't need a special customs form when you use Shipthrifty.com because the customs information prints right on your shipping label. To disclose the items in your package, you'll use proper nouns such as hygiene, snacks, games, etc. The number of items is how many things are in the box; the average value is the average price of each item in the package. (Example: 5 items at $5 apiece is a $25 package.) When doing similar large package quantities, this can be the best estimate and copied on multiple labels. You will want to avoid restricted items to ensure your package is not sent back for violating regulations. Because you create and pay for your shipping label online, you avoid the line at the post office and skip hand entry of your postal clerk's customs information.
Once you complete your shipping details, you will check out. Shipments are paid for by credit card at check out or by mailing a check before shipping. You can also use your credit card to load your My Wallet in the admin area before making shipments. Credit card payments process through Stripe. They have token identification, so card information is not stored directly on our platform. Many large companies use the service (Google, Target, Salesforce, NASDAQ, Shopify, Expedia, etc.)
Just drop it off at a designated drop-off location for the carrier you printed the label for. If you used USPS, you could place it in your mailbox (if it fits), skip the line and put it on the drop-off counter, or schedule a pickup. If you have many packages or larger, heavier ones, we recommend calling your local post office to ensure they can pick up. Pickup availability varies by location and staffing.
