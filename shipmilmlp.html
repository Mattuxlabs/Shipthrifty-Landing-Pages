Reduce Returns
Preflight your address to check the City (APO/FPO/DPO), State (AE, AP, AA), and zip codes with the click of a button to make sure they match against the USPS database, Google, a third-party addressing tool, and our system to detected errors. If we find a discrepancy, a red question mark appears on the right side of that label order. When you click it, a popup displays to identify the error and suggest a correction when you accept the change, the entry updates on the label you are building, and your address book. Military Restricted Items for each zip code is accessible inline with the label so you can double-check the items the military won’t allow. Sometimes things like coffee and over-the-counter meds are not allowed.

Box Count
We make it easy to double-check your numbers with our box count feature. When you’re done entering label details click the box count button and get a summary of packages by name and number of packages for each person.
Manifest For Easy Postal Transfer
When you have multiple packages, your USPS shipments will automatically include a manifest. Print the manifest and give it to the post office when you turn your packages over. All your packages will autoload into the USPS system with a single scan, which will save you countless hours, and the post office will love you!
Track Packages
Get live tracking updates or view the My Tracking page in your account to know exactly where your packages are. You can also enter a tracking number in the tracking field on Shipthrity.com or the corresponding carrier website.

Simple Batch Shipping Tools
Create one to thousands of labels on a single screen that shows your lowest price shipping option first. Since we lay the label sections out like an Excel file, you can see all your entries and edit as you do with a spreadsheet. Double click on a field to change up your details, save an order, and come back to it later, and even export your shipment into an excel file to match up your orders with your packages. The tools automatically number your packages as another checkpoint to ensure you get the right packages with the correct labels.
Exclusive Shortcut Tools
Skip the data entry and have an easy way to save an address to your address book, copy labels, customs forms, and more. The shortcut tools allow you to keep everything on one page, so you do not have to open different views. The buttons above your label table will enable you to do a box count, clear all entries, refresh all rates, and more with the click of a button.
Drop Packages Off or Schedule a Pickups
Either way, enjoy being able to skip the lines because your label is already paid for ahead of time. If you’re shipping to an APO, FPO, or DPO, your customs details are already on your label. Drop it off or use the USPS Schedule A Pickup Form to schedule a pickup. We suggest giving your local post office a call to ensure they can come to get your packages.
Copy Labels and Customs Forms
This feature is in the batch shipping tools, and people LOVE IT. It works excellent if you send more than one package to the same person or the same type of package to multiple people. Copy the labels and customs details as many times as you want with the click of a button and edit any package details as needed.
Automated Addressing
This feature is exclusive to Shipthrifty and helps the groups who send care packages. We call it the Universal Care Package Request Form, which eliminates emailing and texting service member addresses. The form is secure and can be custom-branded for nonprofit organizations. When you share your secure link with your group, others, or post it on your website, a service member’s address can be added to your organization address book automatically. Service members can also complete the form to request packages. The tool helps reduce costly returned packages because it performs an address precheck against the USPS database, Google, a third-party addressing tool, and our system to detect errors. The submitter can fix errors before submitting the form, and the organization gets an email copy of the form as well.

Addressing Mail
Address Basics For Military Mail
Military mail is considered domestic mail and gets assigned special City, State, and Zip codes to identify troops without disclosing their location. Avoid using city or country names in APO/FPO/DPO addresses to prevent mail from entering foreign networks.
City
APO: Army Post Office
Associated with Army or Air Force installations

FPO: Fleet Post Office
Associated with Navy installations and ships

DPO: Diplomatic Post Office
Associated with U.S. diplomatic locations / overseas embassies

MPO: Military Post Office
State
AA: America – Excludes Canada

AP: Pacific – Japan, Korea, Pacific Islands, and Far East

AE: Europe – The Middle East, Africa, and Canada
Zip Code
Europe / Atlantic:
APO / FPO, AE - Zip codes beginning with 090-092 and 094-099
Iraq / Afghanistan / Middle East:
APO / FPO, AE - Zip codes start with 093
Central America / South America/ Caribbean:
APO /FPO, AA - Zip codes beginning with 340
Japan / Korea / Pacific Islands / Far East:
APO / FPO,AP -Zip codes beginning with 962-966
Address Details and Guidelines
Addresses are laid out differently depending on the service branch. The USPS says each shipment to an APO, FPO, or DPO should include the recipients name, rank, and, unit. Mail addressed to soldier or any service member won’t be delivered for security reasons.
TYPICAL MILITARY ADDRESS FORMATS BY BRANCH
Army
Unit [Number] Box [Number]
Air Force
PSC [Number] Box [Number]
Navy
Ship [Number] Hull [Number]
Embassy
Unit [Number] Box [Number]

OUR DEFINING MOMENT
During a casual conversation between friends playing bags, one of our co-founders was asked if we could help a group of military soldiers and their families who were getting deployed. It was an honor to be asked. The request was to make shipping easier for him, his family, and the others in the group who were being deployed. It started out small with this group, grew to be a larger opportunity within a state, and has moved across the country. Since that day, we have found a passion for helping military families and those who support them. There is nothing more rewarding than helping people and organizations do more for those who serve our country. We feel so strongly about this we donate a portion of our proceeds to support military families with JAG approval to donate to Family Plans.
