Shipthrifty can save you money on your major carrier shipping,whether you are sending it to a base or shipping something stateside. Military families and friends keep a little more in their pockets and the nonprofits who ship with us get to stretch their donation dollars.
No Monthly Fee
The Shipthrifty tools are online and free to use from wherever you are. Whether you have a family member who serves or your nonprofit sends care packages to our troops, you unlock all our features when you create your account. We also develop new features based on your needs and suggestions with no passed along costs.
Reduce Returns
Preflight your labels before paying with the click of a button. Shipthrifty will do a check on the City (APO/FPO/DPO), State (AE, AP, AA), and zipcodes to make sure they match against the USPS database, Google, a third party addressing tool, and our system to detected errors. If we detect a discrepancy, a red question mark appears on the right side of that label order. When you click it, a popup displays to identify the error and suggest a correction. When you accept the change, the entry is updated on the label you are building and in your address book. Military Restricted Items for each zip code is accessible inline with the label so you can double-check the items the military won’t allow. Sometimes items like coffee and over-the-counter meds are not allowed.
Audit Control
Box Count
We make it easy to double-check your numbers with our box count feature. When you’re done entering label details click the box count button and get a summary of packages by name and number of packages for each person.
Manifest For Easy Postal Transfer
When you have multiple packages, your USPS shipments will automatically include a manifest. Print the manifest and give it to the post office when you turn your packages over. All your packages will autoload into the USPS system with a single scan, which will save you countless hours, and the post office will love you!
Track Packages
Get live tracking updates or view the My Tracking page in your account to know exactly where your packages are. You can also enter a tracking number in the tracking field on Shipthrity.com or the corresponding carrier website.
Time Saving
Simple Batch Shipping Tools
Create one to thousands of labels on a single screen that shows your lowest price shipping option first. Since we lay the label sections out like an Excel file, you can see all your entries and edit as you do with a spreadsheet. Double click on a field to change up your details, save an order, and come back to it later, and even export your shipment into an excel file to match up your orders with your packages. The tools automatically number your packages as another checkpoint to ensure you get the right packages with the correct labels.
Exclusive Shortcut Tools
Skip the data entry and have an easy way to save an address to your address book, copy labels, customs forms, and more. The shortcut tools allow you to keep everything on one page, so you do not have to open different views. The buttons above your label table will enable you to do a box count, clear all entries, refresh all rates, and more with the click of a button.
Drop Packages Off or Schedule a Pickups
Either way, enjoy being able to skip the lines because your label is already paid for ahead of time. If you’re shipping to an APO, FPO, or DPO, your customs details are already on your label. Drop it off or use the USPS Schedule A Pickup Form to schedule a pickup. We suggest giving your local post office a call to ensure they can come to get your packages.
Copy Labels and Customs Forms
This feature is in the batch shipping tools, and people LOVE IT. It works excellent if you send more than one package to the same person or the same type of package to multiple people. Copy the labels and customs details as many times as you want with the click of a button and edit any package details as needed.
Automated Addressing
This feature is exclusive to Shipthrifty and helps the groups who send care packages. We call it the Universal Care Package Request Form, and it eliminates emailing and texting service member addresses. The form is secure and can be custom-branded nonprofit organization. When you share your secure link with your group, others, or post it on your website a service member’s address can be added to your organization address book automatically. Service members can also complete the form to request packages.
